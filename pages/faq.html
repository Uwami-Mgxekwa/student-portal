FAQ & Support

Find answers to common questions or contact us for help

How do I log in to the student portal?

To log in:

  1. Click the "Click to Login" button on the home page
  2. Enter your Student ID (e.g., GCC2024001)
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Login"

If you don't have an account yet, click "Sign Up Now" to create one.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you forgot your password:

  1. Click the "Still need help?" button below
  2. Select "Password Reset" as the issue type
  3. Provide your Student ID and registered email
  4. Our support team will help you reset your password within 24 hours

My Student ID is not being recognized. What's wrong?

Common reasons and solutions:

  • Not registered yet: Make sure you've completed the signup process
  • Typo in Student ID: Double-check your Student ID spelling and format
  • Case sensitivity: Student IDs are case-insensitive, but make sure there are no extra spaces

If the issue persists, contact support using the form below.

How do I create a new account?

To create an account:

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the home page
  2. Fill in all required fields (Student ID, Name, Email, Phone, Address, Gender, Password)
  3. Check the "I accept all terms & conditions" box
  4. Click "Sign Up"
  5. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard

What should I use as my Student ID?

Your Student ID should be provided by the college administration. It typically follows the format: GCC2024001

If you don't have a Student ID yet, please contact the admissions office.

Can I change my Student ID after registration?

No, Student IDs cannot be changed after registration as they are your unique identifier in the system. If you entered the wrong Student ID, please contact support immediately.

How do I view my class schedule?

To view your schedule:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Schedule" in the sidebar menu
  3. Your weekly timetable will be displayed

Note: Your schedule is based on your enrolled course and certificate type.

Where can I see my enrolled courses?

To view your courses:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Courses" in the sidebar menu
  3. You'll see your enrolled courses, recommended courses, and completed courses

How do I access course materials and assignments?

Course materials and assignments can be accessed through:

  • Courses page: Click on any course card to view details
  • Resources page: Access downloadable materials, forms, and documents
  • Dashboard: View upcoming assignments and deadlines

The portal is not loading. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Refresh the page: Press Ctrl+R (Windows) or Cmd+R (Mac)
  2. Clear browser cache: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete and clear cached data
  3. Try a different browser: Use Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  4. Check internet connection: Make sure you're connected to the internet

If the issue persists, contact support.

Why can't I see my profile information?

If your profile shows "N/A" for some fields:

  • Your academic information might not be set up yet
  • Contact the admissions office to complete your enrollment
  • Make sure you're logged in with the correct Student ID

How do I enable dark mode?

To enable dark mode:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Settings" in the sidebar
  3. Toggle the "Dark Theme" switch
  4. The theme will be saved automatically

How do I update my personal information?

To update your information:

  1. Go to your Profile page
  2. Click the "Edit" button (if available)
  3. Update your details
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Some information like Student ID and course enrollment can only be changed by administration.

How do I sign out of my account?

To sign out:

  1. Click the "Sign Out" button in the sidebar (bottom)
  2. Confirm you want to sign out
  3. You'll be redirected to the home page

Always remember to sign out when using a shared computer!
